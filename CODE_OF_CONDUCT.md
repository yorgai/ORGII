# Code of Conduct

We ask everyone participating in the ORGII community to be respectful, empathetic, and constructive.

## Expected behavior

- Treat others with kindness and professionalism.
- Assume good intent, especially when discussing technical disagreements.
- Give and receive feedback constructively.
- Respect different backgrounds, experience levels, and perspectives.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, threats, or other behavior that makes the community unsafe or unwelcoming is not acceptable.

## Reporting

To report a Code of Conduct violation, email contact@yorg.ai. Reports will be reviewed with care and confidentiality.
